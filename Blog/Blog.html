Adopt A Village . . .

A village saved is a hundred lives saved . Every year due natural causes, urbanization and market fluctuations, tons of farmers end up selling their produce at a significantly lower prices to wholesalers and big companies than the actual price.This begins a cycle of loss which ends up either in abandonment of his land and profession or an unnatural death.  All of this can be prevented with the help of adopt a village campaign.

A farmer in the village of Mirzapur ploughing his paddy field.

How does it work ?

By developing a symbiotic relationship between a corporate group and a village(s), the former provides help with development and improvement of life standards, transport, connectivity, jobs and other facilities. The latter supplies the workforce or human resources. This helps out as reaching out to the community.
